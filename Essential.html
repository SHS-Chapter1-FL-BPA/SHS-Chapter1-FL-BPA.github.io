Dress Code

BPA requires you to wear appropriate clothes that follow their requirements.
Attire should be professional, black suits, black suit skirts, dress shoes with dress socks or dress sandals with heels are required, blue ties only. (Skirt length may be no greater than two inches above the top of the knee, No denim)
Casual clothing is allowed.
Sportswear (jeans), pants, and shirt, t-shirt, and shorts is allowed as well. Footwear is required with all of the above.
For further detail on dress code, click here!

REMEMBER: COVID-19 can spread during these events. Make sure to follow CDC Guidelines at all times.
